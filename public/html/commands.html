Fun General Information Moderation Utility

Commands

Prefixes malil, @malil, *
Messages are cached for 10 seconds and editing them within that time can make a command trigger.


Fun Commands

Fedora

Aliases

  • fedora

Information

  • Puts a fedora on your profile pic or any image you send (doesn't work with links yet)

Example

  • fedora ^
